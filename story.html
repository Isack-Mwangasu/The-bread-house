Who We Are

Traditional Techniques,
Modern Passion

At The Bread House, we believe exceptional bread comes from time-honoured techniques, skilled hands, and the finest natural ingredients. Our artisans blend generations of expertise with methods like long fermentation and hand-shaping to create breads with deep character, aroma, and authenticity.

Since establishing Al Ferdous Bakeries, our mission has been to provide premium, natural bread to the people of Nairobi — ensuring every loaf is free from artificial additives and crafted with cultural pride.

This commitment to craft is why our Arabic Bread stands as one of the best in the market, trusted by restaurants, cafés, hotels, and retailers seeking quality and cultural richness.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Craft with Purpose

To craft exceptional breads and doughs that honour Syrian culinary tradition while embracing innovation — delivering fresh, premium-quality products that elevate the kitchens, menus, and experiences of our partners across Kenya and beyond.

Our Vision

Lead the Region

To become Kenya's leading Middle Eastern–inspired bakery and expand regionally as the benchmark for culturally rich, premium bread craftsmanship — a name synonymous with quality and heritage.
