Our Heritage

From Syria to Nairobi

Bringing generations of artisanal baking expertise to the heart of Kenya.

Traditional Techniques, Modern Passion

At The Bread House, we believe that exceptional bread comes from patience. Our journey is rooted in traditional Syrian techniques, where long fermentation and skilled hand-shaping are not just steps, but a way of life.

Since establishing Al Ferdous Bakeries, our mission has been to provide premium, natural bread to the people of Nairobi, ensuring every loaf is free from artificial additives.
